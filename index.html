About Me

I provide product support for Meyer Sound’s catalog of products including digital audio processing, loudspeakers and electronics. My Primary focus is to provide customers with pre-configured and tested digital systems prior to shipment. I support customers remotely and on jobs/event sites when required and work with customers on planning during all phases of system design/installation, as well as support rental systems and tours. I work closely with Meyer Sound’s Berkeley office, other Technical Support representatives globally, Sales Managers and distributors in numerous global territories that Meyer Sound products are sold.
